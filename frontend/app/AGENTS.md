# PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Vaani AI is a next-generation AI-powered communication coaching platform.

The platform helps users improve:
- confidence
- pronunciation
- tone
- intonation
- pacing
- articulation
- vocal expression
- public speaking

Users upload voice recordings and receive:
- AI-generated feedback
- communication scorecards
- speaking analytics
- progress tracking
- personalized exercises

The platform should feel:
- futuristic
- cinematic
- emotionally intelligent
- immersive
- human-centered
- premium

The experience should feel like:
“the future of human communication”

NOT:
- robotic
- cyberpunk
- crypto
- hacker-style
- overly neon
